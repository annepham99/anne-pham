WHO AM I?






Hi, I'm Anne Pham!

I'm a senior in high school and I love playing music and computer science! My plans for the future is to go to a university and major in computer science and minor in music performance and/or jazz studies. I hope to someday try and integrate computer science and music together, since electronic music is becoming more and more prevalent in this generation. Through my journey, I hope to encourage and inspire other young females to perspire and evolve their own dreams involving music and STEM careers.

In my free time, I love to just sit around and play music. I also love to take photos and wander around the city looking for different viewpoints where I can look out into the city skyline.
